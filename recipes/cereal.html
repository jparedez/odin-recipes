Bowl of Cereal

Description

This recipe is the perfect recipe for anyone who doesn't have time to cook a proper meal in the mornings. Maybe you woke up a little later than usual, or maybe you didn't (we dont judge). Either way, This is a quick, easy, and depending on which cereal you choose, potentially heart healthy meal to start your busy day.

All you need is a couple ingredients, a bowl (and spoon), and an appetite to get started.

Ingredients

  • Cereal of your choice
  • Milk of your choice

That's it!

Steps

  1. Pour your cereal into your bowl. You may want to follow the recommended serving size listed on the nutrition label of the box. Or, if you're like me, pour 'till your heart's content.
  2. Pour your milk until you reach your desired amount.
  3. Grab yourself a spoon and...
  4. Enjoy!
